What are cookies?
Delicous baked treats? Not exactly. Cookies are small bits of information that websites temporarily send to servers while those servers browse the web. At the most basic level, they help us recognize users and keep track of their preferences. Cookies also help us collect anonymous data that helps us make Neevo more enjoyable.
